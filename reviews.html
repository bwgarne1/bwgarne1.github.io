  • Show Name: Waitress
  • Show Date: October 2, 2018
  • Show Location: ASU Gammage, Tempe, AZ
  • Lead Actor/Actress: Christine Dwyer & Bryan Fenkart
  • Reviewer: Bailey

"A phenomenal production effortlessly led by the lead, Christine Dwyer, who plays Jenna with a gentle presence that shows remarkable strength as she slowly grows throughout the show. This show had me from the very first moments. I often found myself laughing uncontrollably only to be thrust back into serious contemplation as we moved from lighthearted themes like Dawn's struggle to find a boyfriend who embraces her quirks to the clearly abusive relationship between Jenna and her husband. This show is a simple story of a woman looking to find a better life while embracing the best parts of her current one, and it truly shows you "What Baking Can Do"! Highly recommended."

  • Show Name: Hamilton
  • Show Date: January 30, 2018
  • Show Location: ASU Gammage, Tempe, AZ
  • Lead Actor/Actress: Austin Scott & Nicholas Christopher
  • Reviewer: Bailey

It was such a pleasure to be in "The Room Where it Happens" and seeing this incredible show come to life. One of the oft overlooked parts of this show is how entrancing the choreography is. I often found myself paying more attention to the incredible choregraphy going on in the background than to the songs I already know so well. This cast did a fantastic job, and I was not at all disappointed even though I have listened to the original Broadway recording hundreds of times. The real strength of Hamilton shines through Lin Manuel Miranda's storytelling, that brings alive even the most boring of historical events into something fascinating for all.

  • Show Name: Wicked
  • Show Date: September 1, 2015
  • Show Location: ASU Gammage, Tempe, AZ
  • Lead Actor/Actress: Alyssa Fox & Carrie St. Louis
  • Reviewer: Bailey

Ah, Wicked! I've always had a soft spot in my heart for this show, and I've seen it several times. This performance at ASU Gammage was one of the best that I've seen. Alyssa Fox as Elphaba truly soars as she belts to the rafters only to come down and make even the softer parts come alive. And who could forget Carrie St. Louis as G(a)linda, who you truly despise at the beginning only to fall in love with as the show goes on. It's telling that the real standout song from this show isn't "Defying Gravity" or the soaring love ballad "As Long as You're Mine", but the late Act 2 duet "For Good". This show truly is about the true love of two best friends. Lovely. It's no wonder this show does so well that it stays for six weeks at a time!

  • Show Name: The Book of Mormon
  • Show Date: May 10, 2018
  • Show Location: Eugene O'Neill Theatre, NYC
  • Lead Actor/Actress: Dave Thomas Brown & Cody Jamison Strand
  • Reviewer: Bailey

I was absolutely blown away by this show! I had doubts going into it because I had no frame of reference for the material - but wow! What a hilarious show; I don't think I stopped laughing except maybe during intermission! I would highly recommend. One tip - don't sit in the front row of the balcony! The lighting equipment causes there to be less leg room in this location! Note that this show is EXTREMELY politically incorrect and is definitely not recommended for younger people - or the easily offended!

  • Show Name: Kinky Boots
  • Show Date: May 8, 2018
  • Show Location: Al Hirschfeld Theatre, NYC
  • Lead Actor/Actress: Tyler Glenn & J. Harrison Ghee
  • Reviewer: Bailey

This show surprised me. I had absolutely no idea what I was getting into when I showed up here, but what I found was an absolutely heartwarming story of acceptance and loving who you are and who you can be. With absolutely irresistibly catchy songs and an incredible message of tolerance, I walked out of this show with a feeling of happiness it's hard to describe. Absolutely a must-see!
