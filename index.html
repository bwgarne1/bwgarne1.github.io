Welcome

Welcome to Musical Mania! This site was created to be your one-stop shop for all things musical theatre. This site was born out of a passion for live musical theatre performances, and a desire to experience all of the greatness that live theatre has to offer. While this site is targeted to theatre fans in the Arizona - especially Phoenix - area, you'll also find lots of valuable information for theatregoers in general!

Within these pages, you'll find reviews of currently running and old performances, currently upcoming performance dates, times, and locations, and lots of tips and tricks for attending live musical theatre performances such as ticketing info, seating suggestions, and proper theatre etiquette.

On the Submissions page, you'll find a form where you can submit content that you'd like to see appear on the site! This is especially great for submitting reviews of any great new shows you've seen recently, or even a show you saw in the past that you'd like to share your opinion on!
